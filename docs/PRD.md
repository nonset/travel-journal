# 🖥️ Project Overview

## Product Name

## Product Type

## Platform

## Description

Travel Journal is a mobile application that helps travelers record:

- Trips
- Expenses
- Photos
- Memories
- Emotions

inside one beautiful timeline.

Unlike traditional expense trackers,

Travel Journal focuses on preserving memories while making expense tracking effortless.

---

Create the world's most enjoyable travel journal.

Travel Journal transforms travel expenses into unforgettable memories.

- Preserve memories beautifully

Make recording travel memories effortless,

beautiful,

# 👥 Target Users

Travel Journal is designed for people who love traveling and want to preserve memories without making expense tracking complicated.

### Primary Users

- Solo Travelers
- Couples
- Friends Traveling Together
- Family Travelers
- Business Travelers

### User Characteristics

- Travel multiple times per year
- Take lots of photos
- Want to remember each trip
- Often forget where money was spent
- Prefer simple interfaces
- Do not like accounting applications

# 😖 User Problems
Many travelers experience the following problems:

- Difficult to remember where money was spent.
- Expense tracking applications feel complicated.
- Photos and expenses are stored separately.
- Receipts are easily lost.
- Memories fade after the trip.
- It is difficult to calculate the total trip budget.
- Travelers cannot easily share their travel memories.
- Existing apps focus only on numbers instead of experiences.
---

# 💡 Solution
Travel Journal solves these problems by combining travel memories, expenses, and daily moments into one simple application.

Key solutions include:

- Easy trip creation
- Beautiful expense timeline
- Photo memories connected with expenses
- Emoji-based emotion journal
- Daily travel notes
- Offline-first experience
- Automatic trip summaries
- Simple and enjoyable interface
